March Mexican Mondays

Looking to spice up your Monday? Why not stop by your local Wetherspoon to enjoy Mexican Monday – just one of the new clubs we’ve introduced to our menu.

From nachos to burritos and quesadillas, our Monday menu brings you a real taste of Mexico, all at your local Wetherspoon. Each dish comes with a drink of your choice* and the option to add extras, such as five-bean chilli or BBQ pulled pork.

About Us

The Food

There are nine food locations at Algonquin College, seven of which are run by Algonquin Food Services. We encourage Algonquin students to tour the campus and taste all the different eateries. Each dining facility has its own unique atmosphere and signature menu items.

The Staff

The mission of the Algonquin College Food Services is to assist the College in the retention of students, increase morale of the entire College campus community, and enhance student life. We do this by providing well balanced nutritional food options to the community at competitive prices. Good nutrition and customer satisfaction are top priorities for Food Services. We always welcome feedback and menu suggestions!
